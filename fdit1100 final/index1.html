Frequently Asked Questions

This program is designed for beginners looking to enter the field of web development, as well as those with some experience who want to enhance their skills.

You will learn:
  • Front-end development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design.
  • Back-end development: Server-side programming, databases, and APIs.
  • Version control with Git.
  • Best practices for creating dynamic and scalable web applications.

No prior experience is required. The program starts with foundational concepts and progresses to more advanced topics.

The program includes:
  • Video tutorials and live coding sessions.
  • Hands-on projects for practical learning.
  • Group discussions and collaborative assignments.
  • Personalized feedback from instructors.

Yes, by the end of the program, you’ll have built fully functional websites and web applications, including both the front-end and back-end.

This program is designed to be completed in 2 years for full-time students, allowing you to gain comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in web development and back-end engineering.

While the program is structured for full-time students, part-time options may be available. Contact the admissions office for more details about flexible schedules.
